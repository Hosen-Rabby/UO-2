GroupValet Community Version

The GroupValet Community Version is the perfect solution for any club, community, house of worship, or any other organization that has a membership roster. Here are just a few of the enhancements you'll enjoy with our Community Version:

Password-free logins

Your members will be able to click a button that will automatically log them in, even if they don't remember their password. This makes it super easy for your members to respond for any event. They just click a button in their email and they are signed up.

Add members to groups, skip the invitation process

In our individual subscription version, members cannot be "added" to groups. For security reasons, they can only be invited to groups. The members need to accept the invitation before they are able to see the group activities and other group features (such as the Wall, Message Center, Photo Gallery, etc). Because our Community Version is based on your roster, we are able to streamline the process and add members to groups, bypassing the invitation process.

Golf Handicaps

We integrate with GHIN, Golfnet and the Chicago District Golf Association to pull in the latest index for all your golfing members. The indexes are automatically updated and included in all rosters and signups.

Bookings

Use our booking calendars to book virtually anything: private lessons, court times, times in the pool, parent/teacher conferences, spa treatments, room reservations, and on, and on…

Group Search

Let your members search for groups they can join throughout your organization. Whether you are in a club, a HOA or a religious institution, our search will help your members quickly and easily connect with each other. This is a great way to introduce new members - they can search for something they are interested in and automatically be included in that group's activities.

Community-Based Ad Campaigns

Embed ads for community events into the activity reminders your members already receive. This allows you to get more eyes on your important events without having to send extra emails. Increase participation and engagement the smart way with GroupValet Ad Campaigns.

Roster-based

Load your roster and set up an automated sync. All groups and members will be controlled by your master roster, so you can rest assured that your organization remains private and secure.

Pricing?

Our annual license cost, paid by your parent club or organization, is based on the number of members you have. Contact us at info@groupvalet.com for more information and a no-obligation demo.
