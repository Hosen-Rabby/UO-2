Pricing

Creating your account is free. If you want to create and manage a group, the following are the pricing options per group:

Monthly: $20 per month per group

Annually: $220 $150 per year per group

Both of the plans listed above come with a 14-day free trial. You can cancel your subscription at any time.


If you are with a country club, community, or other organization that is interested in using GroupValet for your entire organization, please contact us for pricing. Include the number of families in your organization so we can provide accurate pricing to you. Note: if your organization signs up with GroupValet, you will not need the individual group subscriptions listed above.
