PRIVACY POLICY

1. Overview

Oozbop, L.L.C. (collectively, "Oozbop," "we," or "us") are serious about protecting your privacy. We understand your concerns with regard to how information about you is used and shared, and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. That's why Oozbop has established this online privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") for GroupValet.com; (www.groupvalet.com and any replacement and associated sites, the "Site"). This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect about you, how we collect it, how we use it, with whom we may share it, and what choices you have regarding it.

This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and is a part of the Terms of Use of the Site. We encourage you to become familiar with the terms and conditions of both this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use. By accessing and using the Site, you agree that you have read and understand this Privacy Policy, and you accept and consent to the privacy practices (and any uses and disclosures of information about you) that are described in this Privacy Policy.


2. Children Under Age 13

Oozbop does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of thirteen (13). Children are not permitted to use the Site, and Oozbop requests that children under the age of thirteen (13) not submit any personal information to the Site. If you are under thirteen (13) years of age, then please do not use or access the Site at any time or in any manner. Since information regarding children under the age of thirteen (13) is not collected, Oozbop does not knowingly distribute personal information regarding children under the age of thirteen (13). We recommend that minors who are thirteen (13) and over ask their parents for permission before sending any information about themselves to anyone over the Internet.


3. What Information Do We Collect?

We may collect two types of information from or about you in connection with your use of, or submissions to, this Site (collectively, the "Collected Information"): personally Identifiable Information and aggregated information.

Personally Identifiable Information. We may ask you to provide personally identifiable information on the Site, which may include your name, address, telephone number, fax number, company name, e-mail address and relevant details about the product or service that interests you. This information can be gathered when you send us an e-mail or when you complete request forms on the Site. For example, if you request information or register for e-mail updates from us through the Site, you may submit your name and contact information, such as your e-mail address, mailing address or telephone number, to us. If you choose to receive notifications to your mobile phone, you may provide us with your mobile phone number and the name of your mobile service provider. If you post an activity to the Site, you may provide us with information about the activity, the location where the activity will occur, and the individuals who are invited to attend the activity.

In all such cases, you choose whether to provide us with the personally identifiable information. Please understand, however, that certain portions of the Site may not be available to you or we may not be able to respond to your inquiry effectively if you do not provide the personally identifiable information requested.

Aggregated Information. We collect certain anonymous, aggregated data regarding the usage of the Site. This information does not personally identify users, by itself or in combination with other information. We gather it to improve the performance of the Site. The aggregated data we collect can include information such as the type of browser you are using, your computer's Internet protocol ("IP") address, the pages you view, and the length of your visit to the Site. The purposes for which this information is collected and used include facilitating Site operation and system administration, generating aggregate, non-identifiable statistical information, monitoring and analyzing Site traffic and usage patterns, and improving the content and content delivery with regard to the Site and the online media, content, materials, opportunities, and services that we describe or make available on the Site.

In addition, we may retain the content of, and metadata regarding, any correspondence you may have with us or our representatives, regardless of the mode of communication by which such correspondence was made. This information helps us to improve the Site and the online media, content, materials, opportunities, and services that we feature or describe on the Site, and to more effectively and efficiently respond to both current and future inquiries.

As with many other web sites, the web servers used to operate the Site may collect certain data pertaining to you and the equipment and communications method that you use to access the Internet and the Site. For security reasons and to confirm the integrity of our data, we may combine components of this data with other sources of information which may identify you. Unless otherwise described in this Privacy Policy or our Terms of Use, such identifying information will be used solely for our internal business purposes.

Surveys and Contests. We may occasionally request information from you via surveys or contests. Your participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary. The information that we request typically includes your contact details. If you choose to participate, the information that you provide will be shared with the contest/survey sponsors to notify winners and award prizes. Survey information may also be used to monitor and improve the content and performance of the Site.


4. Cookies

Cookies are small bits of data that are deposited on a user's hard drive. We use cookies to monitor individual activity in aggregate to improve the Site. The information we gather may include IP address, user language, the operating system, browser type, the presence/absence of "flash" plug-ins, screen resolution, connection type, and information that identifies the cookie. However, no other user information is generally collected. On most Web browsers you will find a "help" section on the toolbar. This will tell you how to stop accepting cookies, how to be notified when you receive a browser cookie, and how to disable existing browser cookies. Remember though that without browser cookies enabled you may not be able to take advantage of all our Site's features.

We may also use cookies and clear GIFs (also known as "Web beacons" and "1X1 GIFs") to gather and compile such information about you when you return to our Site so that we may better customize our services to you.

In addition to the uses mentioned or described above, or otherwise described in our Terms of Use, we use the information that you submit to us to accomplish the purpose for which it was submitted. For instance, if you contact us by e-mail, we may use the personally identifiable information you provide to answer your question or resolve your problem. We may also use the information that we collect from or about you to send you e-mails, text messages, or other communications to remind you of or provide you information regarding activities, events or other calendar entries. Additionally, we may use the information that we collect from or about you to analyze and improve the content, features, materials, and opportunities that we make available on the Site, to notify you of changes made to the Site and new opportunities made available on or through the Site, to evaluate your needs and customize the Site content delivered to you according to those needs, to send you promotional materials that you request from us, and for other legitimate and lawful business purposes. If you contact us for support or assistance, we may use information that you provide or that we collect about you or your system for purposes such as verifying whether your system meets the minimum requirements needed to use the Site and other various services.

We also use the information collected through the Site for statistical purposes and for improving the Site. We may disclose your personally identifiable information to third parties for tracking purposes, to improve the functionality of the Site, to perform statistical and data analyses, and to deliver e-mails to you from us. Additionally, if you provide Oozbop with content for publishing or feedback, we may publish your user name or other identifying data.

9. How to Unsubscribe from Emails

If you wish to stop receiving emails from GroupValet, please enter your email address into our Unsubscribe Form. You will receive a confirmation email with a link to confirm that you want to unsubscribe. Once you click that link in the confirmation email, you will immediately be unsubscribed from all lists and you will no longer receive any communication from GroupValet. Should you wish to re-subscribe at a later date, simply Contact Us and once we confirm with you over email, we will be happy to turn your notifications back on.
