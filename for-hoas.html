Take the Work Out of Bringing Your Community Together

From Board and Committee Meetings to community events, you can spend a lot of time organizing your community's activities and bringing association members together... GroupValet can automate the entire process for you, handling RSVPs, committee meeting scheduling, and notifying residents of community events -- and send you an automated spreadsheet to let you know who has signed up.

Keep Your Neighborhood in the Loop

Never hear "Oh I wish I'd known!" again. GroupValet makes it easy to keep the whole community in the know…

     • Notify members about upcoming events in the neighborhood…
     • …And let them sign up for the type of notifications they want to receive
     • Take RSVPs and potluck signups for the community barbeque 24 hours a day, no phone calls necessary
     • Customize signups, so you never overbook a catered event
     • Even allow members to book community amenities online in just a few clicks



 

Board and Committee Meetings Made Easy

No more wrangling conflicting schedules, sending reminders, and hunting down confirmations. GroupValet makes it easy to organize and manage board and committee meetings, making meeting easy for organizers and attendees.

     • Poll members to quickly find the best time to meet
     • Automate meeting invites, RSVPs, and reminders
     • Store your documents online, for easy access to minutes, documents, proposals, and more…
        no printing, collating, or stapling required
